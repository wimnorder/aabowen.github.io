About me.


I am a Community Manager focused on helping companies put community in the heart of their operations & become a community-driven business. I am a Developer Evangelist who passionately advocates for developers' needs and help them build their brilliance.

I have a passion for tech, community, design/cities & sustainability. Looking to change the world where those intersect. Currently Head of Developer Relations and Community at DreamFactory Software. I also run a Community Manager Community. Previously Codecademy and SketchUp.

I have a bachelors degree (BEnvd) in Environmental Design and Urban Planning (Community Engagement) from the University of Colorado, Boulder.
